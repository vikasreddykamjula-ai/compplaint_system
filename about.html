About the Online Complaint System

The Online Complaint System is a modern web application designed to simplify the process of raising, tracking, and resolving complaints. It provides a transparent and user-friendly interface for both users and administrators.

  • For Users: Easily submit complaints, track their status, and receive timely updates.
  • For Admins: Efficiently manage, review, and resolve complaints with a streamlined dashboard.

Our mission is to make complaint management seamless, transparent, and accessible for everyone.
