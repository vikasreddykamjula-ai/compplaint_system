Welcome to the Online Complaint System

The Online Complaint System is your one-stop platform for submitting, tracking, and resolving complaints efficiently. Whether you are a student or an administrator, our portal provides a transparent and user-friendly experience.

  • ✔️ Admins can manage, review, and resolve complaints from a central dashboard.
